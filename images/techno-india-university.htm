Techno India University Details

The Techno India University was established in the year 2002. The institution falls under the height of the famous Techno India Group in the whole country. It is the state's one group of this kind that is sanctioned by the NBA and gives credit to AICTE and UGC. Its validity is improved in all domains through the affiants from the Bar Council of India, the Pharmacy Council of India, and the Council of Architecture.
A variety of courses, comprising engineering, science, management, architecture, computer science, pharmacy, humanities, law, nursing, commerce, and design,  are offered by the university's eleven campus schools. Along with this, Techno India University has an association with some of the well-known foreign institutes and is poised to enhance their exposure.

Admission

Here’s the Techno India University admission procedure structured clearly and concisely:

  • Merit and Entrance Exams: Generally, the selection process for Technology for the India University is governed by the merit list and scores in the competitive examinations.
  • Undergraduate Courses: For admission to college undergraduate programs, candidates need to appear for the West Bengal Joint Entrance Exam (WBJEE), or Joint Entrance Exam (JEE Main).
  • Postgraduate Courses: Individuals wanting to get into postgraduate courses should meet the GATE or CAT/MAT/XAT exam requirements.
  • Online Application: The process kicks off with the completion of an online application. Candidates yet should fill in the online application form, which is on the official university website.
  • Submission of Documents and Application Fee: Additionally, candidates should send the required papers and pay the entrance application fee.
  • Evaluation of Applications: After an application is filed, it is reviewed by the institution, which then selects applicants based on their academic standing and results on the admission exam.

Facilities

The facilities at Techno India University contribute to a holistic learning experience, ensuring that students have access to everything they need within the campus.

  • Modern Classrooms:  Classrooms have been outfitted with the latest teaching gadgets and multimedia technology to enhance student interactions.
  • Computer Labs: The campus has spacious and well-furnished computer labs of state-of-the-art with high-speed internet connectivity plus the latest software which gives students practical experience operating in various fields.
  • Healthcare Facilities: The University has a healthcare building on campus for students to use for all their medical concerns and members of staff. Besides regular health check-ups and awareness programs, there are also campaigns such as morning exercises.
  • Transportation: The university gives students and staff public transport. The university bus connects many points in the town through its intricate route net to satisfy the commuters.
  • Gymnasium: For fitness lovers’ the university has a fitness-centric gymnasium. The university also provides effective health promotion activities, regular fitness classes, and yoga classes.
  • Cultural Spaces: The university has game rooms, studios for traditional and contemporary arts, and facilities for cultural performances where students can demonstrate their talents and partake in various cultural activities.
  • Seminar Halls and Conference Rooms: The building consists, of extensive seminar halls and conference rooms, educational workshops, seminars, guest lectures, and meetings are held.

These establishments on campus are the main contributors to holistic education which is availed at Techno India by the students living on the campus. Therefore, everything that students need is located within the campus.
 

Eligibility

Undergraduate Courses
  • Candidates must have passed 10+2 from a recognized board.
  • A minimum aggregate score of 50% is required.
  • The specific subjects required in 10+2 depend on the course. For example, for engineering courses, Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics are typically required.
Postgraduate Courses
  • Candidates must hold a bachelor’s degree in the relevant field from a recognized university.
  • Some courses may require a minimum aggregate score in the bachelor’s degree.
  • Certain courses may also require entrance exam scores such as GATE, CAT, etc.
PhD Programmes
  • Candidates must hold a master’s degree in the relevant field.
  • Admission is often based on the performance in an entrance exam followed by an interview.
  • Some PhD programs may require candidates to have a certain number of years of research or professional experience.
